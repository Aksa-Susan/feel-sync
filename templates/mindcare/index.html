Support for Parents of Children with Dyslexia

What is Dyslexia?

Dyslexia is a learning difficulty that affects reading, writing, and spelling abilities. It is important for parents to understand the signs, interventions, and exercises to help their children.

Signs of Dyslexia

  • Difficulty reading or spelling words correctly.
  • Reversing letters or numbers.
  • Slow or delayed reading skills.
  • Difficulty following written instructions.

What Can Parents Do?

Parents can support their children by providing phonics-based games, regular reading, and building a structured learning environment.
